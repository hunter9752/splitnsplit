Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Netflix Split & Save

Is it legal to share Netflix accounts?

Yes, Netflix officially allows account sharing within a household. Our service helps you manage this sharing efficiently and securely.

How do payments work?

Each member pays their share directly through our secure payment system. The primary account holder receives the combined payment to cover the Netflix subscription.

What happens if someone doesn't pay their share?

Our system automatically notifies members about upcoming payments. If someone fails to pay, their access can be temporarily suspended until payment is received.

Can I change my plan or group members?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. You can also add or remove group members with proper notice.
