Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about splitting Netflix subscriptions

How does Netflix splitting work?

We create Netflix accounts with premium plans and organize trusted groups of members to share the subscription cost. Each member pays only for their portion based on the split size. You'll receive secure access credentials and a dedicated profile or time slot, depending on your split plan.

Is this allowed by Netflix's terms of service?

Netflix allows sharing accounts within a household. Our service organizes shared plans within the bounds of these terms by ensuring responsible usage patterns and limiting concurrent streams as per the plan requirements.

What happens if someone in my split group stops paying?

We handle all payment management. If someone fails to pay, their access is immediately revoked and we replace them with a member from our waitlist. Your access and experience will never be disrupted due to another member's payment issues.

Can I switch between different split plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your split plan at any time, subject to availability. If you want to move to a plan that's currently full, you can join the waitlist and we'll notify you as soon as a slot becomes available.

How do you ensure my personal information is safe?

We never share your personal information with other members of your split group. All payments are processed through our secure payment gateway, and access credentials are provided through encrypted channels. We act as a secure intermediary between all members.
